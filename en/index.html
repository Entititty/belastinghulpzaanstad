◆ Frequently asked questions

Everything you want to know

A preliminary tax refund is a monthly payment from the Belastingdienst (Dutch tax authority) based on an estimate of your tax benefit. For example, if you have mortgage interest deduction, you can receive this benefit monthly instead of once a year after your tax return. This can mean hundreds of euros per month — ideal if you have just bought a house.

Once we have submitted the application for you, the Dutch tax authority usually processes it within 4 to 8 weeks. After that you receive the amount in your account each month. We recommend arranging this as soon as possible after your home purchase.
