◆ About us

A new, local tax office — built honestly

Belastinghulp Zaanstad started in 2026 with one goal: simple, personal help with the Dutch tax return for people in Zaanstad who do not want to fight with DigiD online, and just want someone to sit at the kitchen table with them. We are new — and we are honest about that. We prefer an honest start over a foundation of fake social proof.

Why we started

More and more people get stuck on the Dutch tax return. Not because it really has to be that complicated, but because everything is online, the language used by the Belastingdienst (Dutch tax authority) is hard to follow, and DigiD is a real barrier for many seniors and expats. First-time home buyers often do not know they can receive their tax benefit every month through a preliminary tax refund (voorlopige teruggave).

We wanted an alternative: not a big accountancy chain with waiting times and unclear hourly rates, but a small local office in the Zaanstad municipality that comes to your home, calmly walks through everything with you, and tells you the price upfront.

★ HONEST STORY

New in 2026 — and we are not hiding it

On many websites you see pages full of 5-star reviews from 'Corrie from Zaandam' and 'Henk from Krommenie'. We could have done that too. But that is not an honest picture, and you deserve an honest picture before you trust us with your data.

The truth: we just started this season. Our first clients of 2026 are helping us build real, independent reviews — so they automatically get a €20 discount on every tax return or preliminary refund. No code needed, applied at sign-up.

What we do bring: knowledge of the Dutch tax system, time to calmly explain it to you, and full care for the process from A to Z. And: if you are not satisfied, you do not leave with an invoice — that is our promise.

What we can arrange for you

  • ✓ Income tax return (seniors, first-time buyers, expats)
  • ✓ Preliminary tax refund (voorlopige teruggave) for homeowners
  • ✓ Apply for and check rent benefit (huurtoeslag) and healthcare benefit (zorgtoeslag)
  • ✓ Free filing extension with the Belastingdienst
  • ✓ WOZ objection (property valuation) for Zaanstad residents
  • ✓ Municipal tax waiver (kwijtschelding) applications
