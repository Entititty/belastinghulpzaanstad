Tax help in Koog to de Zaan

Koog to de Zaan is een of the kleinere villages from Zaanstad, located directly on the Zaan river with around 8,000 residents. Questions about tax return in Koog to de Zaan

Ja, zeker. Bewoners from monumentale of historice panden hebben soms specifieke aftrekpossibleheden for onderhoudskosten. We bekijken your situatie and zorgen dat you niets laat liggen. We come simply at your home to de Zaan.

A preliminary tax refund is een monthly payment from the Belastingdienst (Dutch tax authority) based on your verwachte belastingbenefit for the jaar. At een home from €310,000 in Koog to de Zaan scheelt dit around €263 per month who you direct op your account receive.

Once we have submitted the application for you, the Dutch tax authority usually processes it within 4 to 8 weeks. After that you receive the amount in your account each month.

Think of: your DigiD, annual statement(s), WOZ valuation (property tax assessment) from municipality of Zaanstad, annual mortgage statement, bank statements (box 3) and possibly healthcare costs or donations. After you sign up we send you a personal checklist.

We work according to the guidelines of the Dutch Register of Tax Advisers (RB). All our advisers are trained and experienced in Dutch tax law.
