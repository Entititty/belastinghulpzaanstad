Questions about tax return in Wormerland

We helpen alle residents of the municipality Wormerland — in Wormer, Jisp én Neck. De kleine villages Jisp and Neck hebben also entitled to deyourselfde in persone hulp at home. We rijden graag naar you toe, ongeacht welk deel from Wormerland you woont.

A preliminary tax refund is een monthly payment from the Belastingdienst (Dutch tax authority) based on your expecte tax benefit. At een Wormerland-home from €340,000 you receive around €288 per month direct op your account.

Think of: your DigiD, annual statement(s), WOZ valuation (property tax assessment) from municipality Wormerland, annual mortgage statement, bank statements and possibly healthcare costs or donations. After you sign up we send you a personal checklist.

We work according to the guidelines of the Dutch Register of Tax Advisers (RB). All our advisers are trained and experienced in Dutch tax law.
